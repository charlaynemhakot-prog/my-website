More About Nursing Science

Nursing science integrates clinical practice with scientific research to improve patient care. It emphasizes critical thinking, clinical reasoning, and the application of research findings into practice.

Key Areas of Nursing Science:

  • Patient-centered care
  • Health promotion and disease prevention
  • Evidence-based practice
  • Nursing education and leadership
  • Ethics and advocacy in healthcare

Career Opportunities:

  • Registered Nurse (RN)
  • Nurse Practitioner (NP)
  • Nurse Educator
  • Clinical Nurse Specialist
  • Nursing Researcher
